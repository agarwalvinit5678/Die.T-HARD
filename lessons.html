Lesson 1 :The Basics -Food and Calories

What are calories?

A calorie is a unit of energy , like a what is to power . Think of calories as objects in a storage box . Just like how you exchange item "to" and "from" a storage box --calories are a way of keeping track of the energy "in" and "out" of the body's energy budget .

How are calories calculated ?

In any given food item the main components are - Carbohydrates , Proteins and fats which are digestible and fibres which is non digestible . So to calculate calories in any given item all you need to do is Add in the calories of carbs proteins and fats and subtract calorie content of fibres as they are indigestible

Lesson 2 :Calories and nutrients

What Are nutrients ?

The substances used by our body for energy are called Nutrients

Macronutrients

Nutrients that our body needs in large amounts are called macronutrients ....

Micronutrients

And those that our body needs in smaller quantities are called as micronutrients .
  • Carbs are used for quick energy
  • Proteins help &repair tissues
  • Fats stores energy protect cells and send signals in the form of hormones around our body
  • Fibres keep you digestive system healthy
  • Vitamins and minerals are important for various other functions
    • So almost any food you eat has calories in it , which is stored in your body as energy and each component of a meal carries out specific functions .

    Important tips

  • Don't skip fats &carbs in your diet . They play an important role in keeping us fit
  • Choose fibre rich fruits and vegetables daily with your meals .
  • A balanced diet is key to provide the right nutrients to your body

    So where does this Energy go ?

    If you thought the only way you burned calories is through exercise you are in for a surprise ! Energy is used by your body in many ways but primarily for staying alive . You burn about 70-80% of calories that you consume by just staying alive and processing food everyday(Basal metabolic rate (BMR)) . The rest 20% is what is utilized during conscious voluntary activities .

    Fundamental rules of any diet model

      MORE CALORIES IN THAN OUT =WEIGHT GAIN(Caloric Surplus)
      CALORIES OUT THAN IN =WEIGHT LOSS(Caloric Deficit)
    This set of two rules dictate the formation any diet model . All this macronutrient , calorie energy balance sounds like mumbo jumbo to you ? Don't worry here at Die.t_HARD we do all these calculations for you and provide you with a daily meal plan according to your preferences .
